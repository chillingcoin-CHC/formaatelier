About Us

Forma Atelier is a private architectural and interior design studio based in Athens, Greece. Since its founding in 2005, we have delivered elegant, functional, and timeless spaces that celebrate form, light, and materiality.

Derick Richy

Founder & Lead Designer

Highly skilled designer and visionary, Derick has led Forma Atelier since 2005, shaping spaces with a focus on elegance, precision, and creativity. His philosophy centers on refined simplicity, delivering projects that are both beautiful and functional.
