About Us

Forma Atelier is a contemporary architectural and interior design studio based in Kifisia, Athens. We specialize in creating timeless, functional, and emotionally engaging spaces that blend modern aesthetics with authentic Greek architectural sensibilities. From private residences and commercial interiors to full-scale architectural concepts, our work is guided by simplicity, proportion, and the pursuit of refined detail. Every project reflects a balance between form, material, and light — tailored to express each client’s unique vision.

At Forma Atelier, design is more than construction — it’s an artful dialogue between space and emotion, crafted to inspire everyday living.
